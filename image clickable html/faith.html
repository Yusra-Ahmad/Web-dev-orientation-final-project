Faith and Righteousness Calligraphy

Materials

  • Acrylics Paints
  • Canvas
  • Brushes

Description

The calligraphy background depicts a message theme. The canvas was designed with old scroll paper with a quill pen and ink pot. The quill pen is painted in a rainbow shade. The scroll paper shows an ancient style. The background is painted in light blue color to highlight the paper, pen, and ink pot.
