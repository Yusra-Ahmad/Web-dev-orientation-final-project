Wedding Sign Calligraphy

Materials

  • Acrylics Paints
  • Canvas
  • Brushes

Description

The calligraphy is made on a rectangular canvas. The background is painted with five colors. Half of the above part is horizontally painted with white color. The remaining four colors, Red, purple, green, and blue are painted in the other portion. The background is designed with jumbled alphabets. Which enhances the beauty of calligraphy.
