Blessing Calligraphy

Materials

  • Alochol Inks
  • Calligraphy Ink
  • Glossy Sheets
  • Qalam

Description

This “Blessing Calligraphy” is made on a glossy sheet. The corners of the calligraphy are painted with blue and purple alcohol inks with a slight touch of goldish alcohol ink. This calligraphy is written with “Qalam”. This is Sulus Script. Sulus Script is the oldest Arabic calligraphy script.
