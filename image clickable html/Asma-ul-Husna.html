Asma ul Husna Calligraphy

Materials

  • Acrylics Paints
  • Canvas
  • Brushes

Description

The rainbow shades with their tints and dark tones are painted in this calligraphy. The border of this rainbow shade is painted black color. The pattern of the alphabet is written in black color. The beauty of painting using multi shades enhances it more.
