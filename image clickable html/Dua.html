Dua Calligraphy

Materials

  • Acrylics Paints
  • Canvas
  • Brushes

Description

The “Dua Calligraphy” is made on canvas. The background of the calligraphy is painted with brown color. One side of the canvas is covered with jumbled Arabic alphabets in circles. The sketch of hands depicts they are praying. The calligraphy is written with a brush.
