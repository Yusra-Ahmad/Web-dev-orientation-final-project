Architecture Painting

Materials

  • Acrylics Paints
  • Canvas
  • Brushes

Description

The painting is a small-scale architectural piece that features a vibrant color scheme of pink, red, and blue.The use of pink and red colors adds a sense of liveliness and energy to the painting. The pink color dominates the composition, creating a bold and eye-catching effect. The red color is used sparingly to add warmth and depth to the painting, while the blue color is used to create a sense of coolness and calmness.The small size of the painting adds to its charm and makes it perfect for adding a pop of color to any small space.
