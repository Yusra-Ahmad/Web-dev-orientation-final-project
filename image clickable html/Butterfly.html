Butterfly Painting

Materials

  • Acrylics Paints
  • Canvas
  • Brushes

Description

A blue butterfly painting depicts a beautiful butterfly with its wings with outer gold leafing layer. The butterfly has a bright blue color, with a bit of white and black lines. The background is painted with blue and white abstract form. The stroke of the brush used in this painting is light and delicate, creating a sense of movement and grace. This painting's overall effect is calming and soothing, just like the gentle flutter of a butterfly's wings.
